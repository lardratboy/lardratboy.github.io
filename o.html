OpenJRPG · Round-Based

Element Cycle

Fire 🔥 → beats Air 🌪 → beats Earth ⛰ → beats Water 💧 → beats Fire 🔥
Light ☀ ↔ Dark 🌙 (mutually super)
Same element = 0.5× resist. Off-cycle = 1×.

Status

🔥 Burn — chip dmg + halves Attack
☠ Poison — chip damage / turn
⚡ Paralyze — half speed, may skip turn
💤 Sleep — skip turn until hit

Rows

▶ Front — full physical damage in/out
◁ Back — half physical in/out (magic unaffected)

Bosses

Every 3rd battle. Multi-phase scripted AI with telegraphs.
⚠ = boss is charging an attack. Defend or burst it down.
